# Deer Hunt - E-commerce Platform

## Project Purpose
Deer Hunt is an e-commerce web application designed for selling fashion and lifestyle products. The platform provides a modern shopping experience with product browsing, cart functionality, and administrative management capabilities.

## Key Features
- **Product Catalog**: Display of various fashion items including shoes, sunglasses, watches, pants, jackets, and t-shirts
- **Shopping Cart**: Add to cart functionality with price tracking and item counter
- **Brand Navigation**: Sidebar navigation featuring popular brands (Jordan, one8, Puma, Adidas, Nike)
- **Category Filtering**: Product categorization for easy browsing
- **Admin Panel**: Administrative interface for product and inventory management
- **Responsive Design**: Bootstrap-based responsive layout for mobile and desktop
- **Search Functionality**: Product search capability in the main navigation
- **Pricing Display**: Clear pricing with discount information and promotional offers

## Target Users
- **Customers**: Fashion-conscious shoppers looking for branded clothing and accessories
- **Administrators**: Store managers and staff who need to manage products, categories, and orders
- **Guest Users**: Visitors who can browse products before registration

## Use Cases
- Browse and search for fashion products by category or brand
- Add items to shopping cart and track total price
- View detailed product information and pricing
- Administrative management of products, categories, and brands
- User authentication and account management
- Order processing and payment handling
